How has my music changed during lockdown?

Login with Spotify

We analyse your most played songs from the past few months to see how your music has changed. Seven aspects of your music are tracked:

  • Positivity: Shows if your music has become more or less positive
  • Danceability: Shows if your music taste has become more suited to dancing
  • Energy: Shows how much faster and louder the music has become
  • Vocals: Shows how the amount of vocals in your songs has changed
  • Acousticness: Shows how acoustic your music is
  • Boringness: Shows how boring your music has become. This is based on tempo, loudness, and energy
  • Variety: Shows if your music has become more or less varied
